About Us

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate data scientists, DataFlask Lab began as a small research initiative focused on machine learning applications. Over the years, we've grown into a leading interdisciplinary research center with collaborations across academia and industry.

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of data science through rigorous research, innovative applications, and open collaboration. We believe in making data science accessible and impactful across all sectors of society.
